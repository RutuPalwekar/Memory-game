How to Play:

Click on any card to flip it over and reveal the fruit. Then, click on another card to find its match. If the cards match, they will disappear. If they do not match, they will flip back over. The game ends when all cards are matched. Try to complete the game with the fewest moves and in the shortest time possible. Good luck!
